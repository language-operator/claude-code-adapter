claude-code LanguageAgentRuntime installed.

Prerequisite: the `language-operator` chart must be installed first (it provides
the LanguageAgentRuntime CRD). Installing against a cluster without the CRD fails.

The runtime is cluster-scoped and can be referenced by LanguageAgents in any
namespace via spec.runtime:

  apiVersion: langop.io/v1alpha1
  kind: LanguageAgent
  metadata:
    name: my-agent
  spec:
    runtime: claude-code

Image: {{ .Values.image.repository }}:{{ .Values.image.tag }}
